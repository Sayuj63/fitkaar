Kapdo ka Kalakar

India’s first-ever fully customizable clothing brand, where we design specially for you. Proudly women-led and bootstrapped.

What started as a college passion project, born out of a shared love for designing costumes for their dance society, soon turned into Fitkaar. With a growing team that helps bring your ideas to life, they continue to build a brand that makes fashion feel personal.

It all began in college. We were both part of the dance society, Suhani as the Vice President and Mitanshi as the Creative Head.

When it was time to plan our seniors’ farewell, we wanted to gift them something personal, something that felt like them.

That’s when the idea struck, personalised t-shirts with elements each senior loved.

After going through a lot of drafts and revisions, we finally had our first set of designs ready.

When we shared them with our batch, the responses poured in, 'you guys should start something together!'

Seeing our seniors’ reactions when they opened their tees was everything, it genuinely made us realise how special personalised designs can feel.

That same year, we also designed the costumes for our dance society.

One day, we decided to take this passion project forward- we met, planned, and that’s where Fitkaar began.

From designing for our seniors to now seeing so many of you wear what we create, it’s been the most exciting and fulfilling journey, and we’re just getting started.
